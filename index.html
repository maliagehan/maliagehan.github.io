About

I am a NSF-PGRP postdoctoral fellow in Todd C. Mockler's laboratory at the Donald Danforth Plant Science Center. I work with the model C3 grass Brachypodium distachyon, and the model C4 grass Setaria viridis. I am primarily interested in mechanisms and natural variation of abiotic stress resistance and I utilize high-throughput phenotyping and genomics tools. I am very interested in education and outreach and have helped establish a Maker Group at the Donald Danforth Plant Science Center.
